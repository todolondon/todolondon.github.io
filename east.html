East London

East London is well-known for its dedication to all kinds of niche music and art.

brilliant corners is a Japanese restaurant with famous DJs often playing. Find out for yourself:


The Vikor Wynd Museum of Curiosities, Fine Art and Natural History is a museum of all things unusual, from taxidermy to Happy Meal toys all in one place. Go explore with a cup of tea or a cocktail.


Present & Correct is a lovely shop with all kinds of very designer stationery which was handpicked by the owners (designers, of course).


Barbican is the largest European venue dedicated to arts (as well as a building complex with really pretty flats, an indoor conservatory open to the public on weekends, a cinema...)
