North London

North London is famous for its parties. It also has plenty of cool museums.


Wellcome Collection is a free museum and art collection dedicated to health, science, and medicine. It also has a lovely, comfortable library open to the public.


Have you ever wondered what psychoanalysis is? Find out in the Freud Museum, based in what used to be the actual home of the late Freud family.
