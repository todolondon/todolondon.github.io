South London

South London is famous for its art students and laid-back vibe (unless it's wealthy Chelsea, that is).


Chelsea Physic Garden is the oldest botanic garden in London, with over 5000 plants in its collection.


Victoria and Albert Museum is the leading museum of art and design. While it houses a fantastic collection of fashion, photography, furniture and many other objects, its recent exhibition was for example on Winnie-the-Pooh.

South London Gallery is a gallery of contemporary art. What is particularly worth exploring? A great choice of books and niche magazines on art and design in the gallery shop, as well as the gallery green space, Orozco Garden.


Fashion and Textile Museum is a colourful (literally) museum dedicated to fashion, textiles, and crazy clothing. Right now it is showing an exhibition on the history of t-shirts.
