WEST LONDON

West London is not just Oxford Street!


The Kyoto Gardens

The Kyoto Gardens in Holland Park has been a unique place on the London map (of parks) since 1991. A great relaxing space for all Japanophiles (or fans of peacocks).


Pollock’s Toy Museum

A short walk from Oxford Street, Pollock’s Toy Museum is a space dedicated to toys, the slightly forgotten toy theatre in particular.


Choccywoccydoodah

Choccywoccydoodah, beyond its crazy name, offers a range of equally crazy handmade sugary delights (in the photo here, the whole display is made of chocolate!).


sketch

sketch is a restaurant with extremely Instagram-friendly fluffy pink interior. “Vogue” described it as a great place of tea “with a twist”.
